Features

Lobby

Our lobby is designed by skaters, for skaters, we have provided spots to hold your boards while checking in and out and a "skate friendly" lobby, meaning you're welcome to ride around. You don't have to run from the security guards here!

Halls

Our hallways are all paved with concrete and the walls are designed with beautiful patterns and pictures. The hallways were made so you can skate to your room if you would like. We want your stay to be as skater friendly as possible!

Rooms

We have rooms for one and two people, the smaller rooms contains one bed, a small bathroom, a shower, and a place to store and fix your board. The rooms for two people have twin beds, a bathroom, a shower, and a large desk to fix your boards.

Benefits

We provide breakfast included in the booking, as well as tools, hardware, wax, and helmets free of charge while you stay. You must have a room booked to skateboard inside and outside of our hotel, sorry, this is not your average skatepark!

In Depth

We offer top of the line rooms with the most comfortable beds you will sleep in. The rooms are the perfect size for one or two depending on how many guests. Each room contains a work bench with all the skateboarding tools and materials you will need to fix up your board whether you're a night owl or an early bird. Each floor has concrete hallways making it easy to acces by skateboard and following our theme of being skater friendly. Don't worry though, when you get to your room, a soft carpet will be waiting for your aching feet after a long day of skateboading.

Stay A While

You can book directy on our website by clicking the button to the right. It will show you the different selection of rooms, prices, and various other fees. While you stay you are welcome to enjoy our incredibly designed hotel by skateboarding inside and out. Room & Board was opened by skaters, for skaters!
